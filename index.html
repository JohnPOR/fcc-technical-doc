About this doc.

This is a technical document designed to meet the requirements of freecodecamp's 'technical documenatation' challenge, as a requirement for their front-end certificate.

For this project I decided to utilise solutions to common JS problems, provided courtesy of Traversy Media's JS Cardio Sessions. None of these solutions are my own, and my use of them is purely presentational for the sake of this fcc challenge. All rights and praise belong to Brad Traversy at Traversy Media, who's content I have been using alongside that provided by the awesome team at fcc.
